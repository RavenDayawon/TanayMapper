About

Welcome to the Tanay Establishment Mapper! This application is designed to help users explore and locate various establishments in Tanay, Rizal. From diners to schools, we aim to provide you with a map with their locations for easy navigation and discovery.

How to Use

  • Use the toggles to filter categories like diners, schools, and more.
  • Click on map markers to view details about each establishment.
  • Zoom and pan around the map to explore the area.
